## FAQ Content

**Q: Do you work on diesel vehicles?**
Yes, diesel repair is our specialty. We service diesel trucks and heavy equipment of all makes and models. If it runs on diesel, we have the tools and training to diagnose and fix it.

**Q: Do you service all makes and models of cars and trucks?**
Yes. We service gas and diesel vehicles of all makes and models. Whether you drive a domestic truck or an imported car, our technicians have the equipment and expertise to help.

**Q: Do you install remote starters?**
Yes. We install remote start systems including the Astro Start 2 Way RSS S5225. Call us to ask about scheduling an installation appointment.

**Q: Do you install ignition interlocks (breathalyzers)?**
Yes, we install and service breathalyzer and ignition interlock devices. Call (218) 773-7809 to schedule.

**Q: What are your hours?**
We are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Saturday appointments are available by appointment only. We are closed on Sunday.

**Q: Do you serve customers in Grand Forks, ND?**
Yes. We are located just across the river in East Grand Forks, MN, and we regularly serve customers from Grand Forks, ND. We also serve customers in Crookston, MN.

**Q: How do you decide what my vehicle needs?**
We start with a complete inspection and use computer diagnostics to pinpoint the problem before recommending any repairs. We walk through the findings with you and give you an honest assessment so you can make an informed decision.

**Q: Are you a family-owned business?**
Yes. Driscoll's Auto Service was founded by Kirk Driscoll, who grew up farming in East Grand Forks before building the shop into a community staple. Kirk passed away in October 2025, and his son Kaleb now carries the business forward with the same work ethic and values.
